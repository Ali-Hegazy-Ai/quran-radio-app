# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, ethnicity, nationality, or level of experience.

This is a project built for the Muslim community. We ask all participants to maintain Islamic etiquette (adab) in all interactions.

## Our Standards

**Examples of positive behavior:**
- Using respectful and kind language
- Being patient with others
- Providing constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Disrespectful conduct toward Islamic content or values
- Spam, trolling, or off-topic disruptions

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
